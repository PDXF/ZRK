Frequently Asked Questions - BadUSB Free Services

What are BadUSB free services?
BadUSB free services are security measures and tools designed to protect against BadUSB attacks, which exploit vulnerabilities in USB devices. These services help identify, prevent, and mitigate potential threats.
How can I protect my devices from BadUSB attacks?
To protect your devices, use our BadUSB detection software, keep your systems updated, disable autorun for USB devices, and only use trusted USB devices. Our free services provide additional layers of protection and guidance.
Are your BadUSB free services compatible with all operating systems?
Our free services are compatible with most major operating systems, including Windows, macOS, and Linux. Check our compatibility list for specific versions and requirements.
How often should I scan my devices for BadUSB threats?
We recommend running a scan at least once a week and immediately after connecting any new or unfamiliar USB devices. Our free service includes automated scheduled scans for your convenience.
